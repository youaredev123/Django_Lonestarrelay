Experience your own epic victory at the Texas Independence Relay! Your ~200 mile journey traces the route of Sam Houston and the Texian army, beginning in historic Gonzales (home of the Come & Take It skirmish) to the site of the storied final battle for Texas' Independence at the San Jacinto Monument!
