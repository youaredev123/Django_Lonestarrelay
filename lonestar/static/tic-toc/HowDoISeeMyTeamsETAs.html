How do I see my team's ETAs?

To see your team's ETAs (projected future exchange times based on the team's current overall pace):
1) Either click on the Team Name links found in the team's infobox;
2) Or, click on the Team's name within the scrolling team menu (located at the bottom right corner of the map.)
